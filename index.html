Text tool commands

To edit the text, click the element and then type.
Double-click the text to open the text color selector.
Double-click the text to open the text font selector.
You may move the text by clicking and holding down on it, then drag it it its new home.
Use your mouse's scroll wheel to resize your text.
Use the UP arrow key to rotate the text clockwise, and DOWN for anti-clockwise.
You may either right-click the text, or remove all text in the element to remove it.

Modes

In this mode, your brush is off and the eraser away. You are simply admiring your masterpiece. Press V on your keyboard to use this mode.
Draw away. Move your cursor around to draw. Press D on your keyboard to use this mode, or left-click within the canvas to toggle.
Undo. Move your mouse around to remove items under the cursor. Press E on your keyboard to use this mode, or right-click within the canvas to toggle.
